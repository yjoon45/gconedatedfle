FAQ FAQ

Q: What are “Date Flows”?
A: I’ve polled our readers for the most in-demand date types that guys want to know about. I then created a STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE to running each kind of date… from how to get the date, to how to plan it, to how to seduce a girl once she’s on it and get to intimacy. There’ve never been such methodical, step-by-step, EFFECTIVE guides to specific kinds of dates like these ever before (and I doubt there will be ever again. This stuff takes a lot of work to put together!).
Q: Will there be more Date Flows?
A: Yes. I have a list of 20 potential Date Flow dates to create. The Home Date and Walking Date were among the most popular, so I created those first (released in 2022). This time, I’m making two more available: the Café Date and Stargazing Date. Upcoming Date Flows for future limited release include: the Park Date, the Game Date, the Dancing Date, the Beach Date, and the Day Trip Date.
Q: Why are the Date Flows only available for a limited time?
A: I wanted to create something fun and unique to offer One Date owners with a collectible element to it and limited availability. For the foreseeable future, each Date Flow will only be available during its release period, and only for new buyers or previous buyers who pick it up while it’s available.
Q: What kind of information is in a Date Flow?
A: I’ve broken each Date Flow into discrete sections: Introduction, When to Use, Who It’s For, Seed the Date, Ask Her Out, Plan the Date (also includes “Running the Date”), Success Tips, Follow-Up After the Date, and Wrap Up. I shot each date flow as a 30-40 minute video lesson based on a tight outline, and I’ve created companion ebooks, written by me, for each Date Flow. I’ve even answered questions from readers about each date. There’s little left to the imagination here – these guides to each type of date are complete, “hello” to “moan” date walkthroughs.
Q: If I miss a Limited Edition Date Flows during the Limited Release, can I pick it up later.
A: No. So if you want it, grab it now!
